Parad0x Compress Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-11

Summary

Parad0x Compress is a media compression app. We aim to collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Data We Collect

  • Media files: We process the files you choose to compress. Your files are handled on-device unless a feature explicitly requires uploading.
  • Diagnostics (optional): If enabled, we may collect crash logs and basic diagnostics to improve stability.
  • Purchases: If you buy subscriptions or lifetime access, Apple processes the payment. We do not receive your card details.

How We Use Data

  • To compress your selected media and documents.
  • To troubleshoot crashes and improve performance (if diagnostics are enabled).
  • To verify Pro entitlements (subscription/lifetime access).

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties except where needed to operate the app (for example, Apple for purchases, and service providers for diagnostics if enabled).

Retention

Your selected media remains on your device. Diagnostics data (if enabled) is retained only as long as needed to investigate issues.

Your Choices

  • You choose what files to compress and where to save/export results.
  • You can disable diagnostics collection (if offered in-app or in system settings).
  • You can contact support at: Help@parad0xlabs.com
