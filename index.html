FAQ

  • Why didn’t my file get smaller?
    Some files are already compressed. Try a stronger compression level or a different output format.
  • Where are compressed files saved?
    Files are saved to your chosen location via the share/save flow.
  • Why does AVIF take longer?
    AVIF can deliver excellent quality at smaller sizes, but it may take longer to encode on some devices.
  • PDF compression not changing size?
    Some PDFs are already optimized, or they contain scanned images that have limited savings.

Troubleshooting

  • If compression fails, try restarting the app and re-selecting the file.
  • Make sure you have enough free storage for temporary processing.
  • If a file is extremely large, try compressing in smaller batches.
